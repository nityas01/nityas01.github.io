Recipe of the Day: Spaghetti Carbonara

This classic Italian dish is a quick and easy dinner that's sure to please. Follow these steps to make a delicious carbonara!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti - 400g
  • Pancetta - 150g
  • Eggs - 4 large
  • Grated Parmesan cheese - 50g
  • Salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. Fry the pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with grated Parmesan cheese.
  4. Drain the pasta and quickly toss with pancetta and the egg-cheese mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and serve hot.
